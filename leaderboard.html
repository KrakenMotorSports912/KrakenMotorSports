HOW RANKING WORKS

Submission Rules

  • Clean lap only (no major penalties)
  • One screenshot/video proof required
  • Track, car, and game must match event
  • Admins verify before publishing

Supported Games

  • Assetto Corsa (all versions)
  • Assetto Corsa Competizione
  • F1 2025
  • Forza Motorsport / Horizon

Ranking Logic

  • Lower lap time = higher rank
  • Event-specific and global boards
  • Tie-breaker by earliest verified time
  • Rejected entries include a reason
